I Come From The Net.

Web Developer

Need a developer to build your next exciting web project? If your here considering to hire me keep reading.

I am by trade a LAMP (Linux Apache MySQL and PHP) developer. I consider myself proficient in designing, implementing and maintaining web applications.

I have experience with many php frameworks including Symfony2, Codeigniter, Zend and FuelPHP.

I am based and educated in Sydney, Australia. Outside of paid employment I have developed a number of projects hosted on github, highlights include:

  1. Faker - A Comprehensive test data generator based on Doctrine DBAL and Synfony2 Components.
  2. Migrations - A Schema Manager for php based on Doctrine DBAL and Symfony2 Components.
  3. LaterJob - A database queue library with metrics for shared hosts.

These projects demonstrate my skill and education and experience.

I am a proponent of the Open Web and believe it is the responsibility of enterprises to contribute back to the projects that underpin their businesses and more importantly enterprises should not lock away their users data.

I attended the University of Western Sydney during 2005-2010 obtaining a Bachelor of Computing majoring in web-development followed by a year of postgraduate study. On an earlier career trajectory during 2000-2002 I attended a locale training institution (NSW TAFE) obtaining a Diploma of Business (indulging my love of finance) and before commencing university employed those skills in administrative postions for 3 years.

After finishing university start of 2011 I began freelancing and contract work eventually finding my way to a startup developing web applications for financial services industry however this ceased at the begining of 2012.

After a brief respite during 2012 I am once again pounding the pavements looking for ideas to grow into successful enterprises.
