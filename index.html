Web Developer

Need a developer to build your next exciting web project. Looking to design an application?

I am by trade a LAMP (Linux Apache MySQL and PHP) developer. I consider myself proficient in designing, implementing and maintaining web applications.

I have experience with many php frameworks including Symfony2, Codeigniter, Zend and FuelPHP.

I am based and educated in Sydney Australia and have a love of cool weather, listening to piano rock and being bemused by the ever worsening state of genre movies.

Outside of paid employment I have developed two complete and complex projects hosted on github:

  1. Faker - A Comprehensive test data generator based on Doctrine DBAL and Synfony2 Components
  2. Migrations - A Schema Manager for php based on Doctrine DBAL and Symfony2 Components

These two projects are good demonstrations of the skill and insight that I bring.

I am a proponent of the Open Web and consider it a responsibility to support products and services that use and contribute back to FOSS software that powers their enterprises.

I attended the University of Western Sydney during 2005-2010 obtaining a Bachelor of Computing majoring in web-development followed by a year of postgraduate study. While on an earlier career trajectory I attended a locale training institution (NSW TAFE) 2001-2002 obtaining a Diploma of Business (indulging my love of balance sheets) and before commencing university employed those skills in administrative work for 3 years.

After finishing university start of 2011 I freelanced during which made contacts leading to a startup developing web applications for financial services industry closing end of 2011.

After a brief respite during 2012 I am once again pounding the pavements looking for ideas to grow into successful enterprises.
